About Us

Welcome to CoRe

A platform designed to assist incoming freshmen at PHINMA University of Iloilo in making informed decisions about their college courses.
Our application guides senior high school students through a series of personalized questions to help them discover the courses that align with their interests, skills, and potential career paths. Rather than relying solely on grades, CoRe delves deeper, providing recommendations that reflect students’ passions and abilities.

At CoRe, we aim to simplify the decision-making process, ensuring that every student finds a course that’s the best fit for their future. Whether you're leaning towards Information Technology, Business Administration, Criminology, or any other field, CoRe is here to help you navigate your educational journey with confidence.
